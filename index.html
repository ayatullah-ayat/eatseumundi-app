EATS was established in Eumundi QLD Australia in 1989. Since then we have been striving to provide the freshest, yummiest classics you, your friends and your entire family can enjoy. There is something for everyone at EATS. Using fresh, local ingredient, our meals and drinks at EATS will not disappoint. Come on in a join our loyalty program which offers huge discounts, surprise gifts, event accountments and every 7th coffee on us!

As we strive to grow our locations throughout Australia we also have a very special project in South America under development. This will be our 1 for 1, where 1 meal sold in Australia will be one meal gifted to someone in need.

We will partner up our social enterprise project with education leaders and will offer educational experience for those who wish to start their micro business. We are co-developing a micro-finance arm to ensure long lasting positive change can be made for communities.
